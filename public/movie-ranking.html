The Best Movies According to Carlos Irias

My Top 3 Movies of all times

Thor: Love and Thunder

Thor: Love and Thunder captivated me with its perfect mix of action, humour, and heart, delivering an epic yet deeply personal superhero adventure.

Tears of the Sun

Tears of the Sun gripped me with its intense action and raw emotion, portraying the harrowing realities of war and the profound impact of sacrifice

The Pianist

The Pianist moved me deeply with its haunting portrayal of survival, resilience, and the enduring power of music amidst the horrors of war.
